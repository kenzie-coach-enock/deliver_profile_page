What is the IT Field?

The IT field, or Information Technology field, is responsible for managing the flow of information in a computer network of an organization.

This information, more commonly known as data, is managed through software, databases, hardware, security networks, and other elements that make up the scope of a technology professional's work.

What Does an IT Professional Do?

An IT professional's mission is to ensure the functioning of the technologies necessary for a good flow of information in computer networks.

To achieve this, they can create solutions as a web developer; maintain systems as a DevOps; analyze data to promote improvements as a big data analyst, among others.
